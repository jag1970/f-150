Asakusa

Asakusa | Random Street | Tokyo

Asakusa is a small district in Tokyo, Japan. It was on of the first locations we visited when we first arrived in Tokyo. Our Hotel was nearby and when we first arrived it was dark however, when we first set out the following morning, this is one of the first pictures I took. It is a popular area for shopping with the famous temple Senso-ji nearby and tons of shopping. You will also notice Tokyo Skytree in the background.

Tokyo Skytree

Skytree | Sumida | Tokyo |

The Toyko Skytree is one of two towers that embrace the Tokyo skyline. The other being the Tokyo Tower which is older. It is a very popular tourist destination and is right next to a major shopping mall. In the forefront is the Asahi Beer Hall. Asahi is probably the most common made beers in Japan. You will also notice an odd golden structure that is also nicknamed "Golden Turd" for obvious reason.

Sumida River

Sumida River | Sumida | Tokyo

The Sumida River is a river that runs through central Tokyo. Its a beautiful river that has a total of 26 bridges crossing over. It is also a popular tourist destination that you can take a taxi boat type ride down. We actually did this and witnessed many beautiful sites. At the end of the boat ride, we hike to the other tower in Japan so be basically travel from one tower to the other by boat.

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower | Minato | Tokyo

Tokyo Tower is another popular observation tower in Tokyo. It is actually older than the Skytree Tower which was built in 2012 and Tokyo Tower being made in 1958. The Tokyo Tower is much more iconic. From here we visted the Zojo-Ji Temple in the forefront. Interesting fact, this temple is one of the locatons "The Wolverine" was filmed.

Enoshima Shrine

Enoshima Shrine | Enoshima Island | Kanagawa

Here is the entrance to the beautiful Enoshima Shrine on the Enoshima Island. This tiny island is located in Kanagawa Japan. Kanagawa is without a doubt one of my favorite places on the planet. Beautiful beaches by comfortable small towns. I place I would love to retire in if I could. Kanagawa is also popular for the famous painting of the Kanagawa wave. On a clear day, you can also see Mt Fuji

Osaka Castle

Osaka Castle | Osaka | Japan

Osaka castle is an amazing castle in Osaka Japan. The Castle is over 300 years old and was built around 1585. Around the castle is a large rampart surrounded by a moat. The most castles, this severed as a protection. Around the moat, the castle is surrounded by lush gardens. It is a must see if visiting Osaka and is one of my favorite site visited. Osaka is about two hours away from Tokyo by bullet train.
